    • Step 1: Login

    You have to log in to your OurHome account, if you don't have one, you are required to register to continue the process. With OurHome, our agents provide all the support you need through the whole selling process. All these require an account.

    Step 2: Upload

    Once logged in, you have to upload information regarding ownership of your property, including but not limited to proof of land record, MLS number(If you currently have one) etc. All relevant documents will be used to expedite the review process.

    Step 3: Review

    After you successfully upload all documents, OurHome will review all documents you uploaded to confirm the ownership and determine the status of the house. If you don't have an MLS number, we will give you one once your information is verified.

    Step 4: Appointment

    Once your documents are finalized, OurHome will prepare an comparative market analysis. After that, OurHome will provide more services regarding your selling process, such as managing all the incoming calls about your home and arranging showings.
